Verb to be i used for _three particular_ situations.

- Descriptions of _persons_ or _objects_.
- Characteristics.
- Present Situations.

---
### Ways to use Verb To-Be

1. I _am_ Pablo.
2. She _is_ tall.
3. He _is_ Jess.
4. It _is_ a Car.
5. You _are_ (a) students.
6. We _are_ friends.
7. They _are_ happy.

_am_ -> **i**
_is_ -> **he, she, it**
_are_ -> **you, we, they**

---
### Contractions

**Las contracciones aportan naturalidad al discurso** y son especialmente comunes en el inglés hablado. Algunos beneficios de su uso incluyen:

- Hacen que tu inglés suene más natural y menos formal.
- Facilitan la fluidez en la conversación.
- Son esenciales en el inglés cotidiano.

_Examples_:

I am Jhon -> I'_m_ Jhon.

She is tall -> She'_s_ tall.
He is Jess -> He'_s_ Jess.
It is a car -> It'_s_ a car.

You are students -> You'_re_ students.
We are friends -> We'_re_ friends.
They are happy -> They'_re_ happy.

---
### Negative Sentences and Contractions

I -> I am not:
- _I'm not_
He/She/It -> He/She/It is not:
- _He's not_ or _He isn't_
- _She's not_ or _She isn't_
- _It's not_ or _It isn't_
You/We/They -> You/We/They are not:
- _You're not_ o _You aren't_
- _We're not_ o _We aren't_
- _They're not_ o _They aren't_

---
### Questions

Para transformar una oración afirmativa en pregunta con el verbo _to be_, es necesario seguir **dos reglas fundamentales**:

- Invertir el orden de los elementos: el verbo _to be_ se coloca al inicio de la oración.
- Utilizar la entonación ascendente y el signo de interrogación al final.

I _am_ -> _Am_ I ...? -> _Yes, you are / No, you are not_
She _is_ ... -> _Is_ she ...? ->  _Yes, she is / No, she is not_
He _is_ ... -> _Is_ he ...? -> _es, he is / No, he is not_
It _is_ ... -> _Is_ it ...? -> _Yes, it is / No, it is not_
You _are_ ... -> _Are_ you ...? 
	-> _Yes, I am / No, I am not_ (singular)
	-> _Yes, we are / No, we are not_ (plural)
We _are_ ... -> _Are_ we ...? -> _Yes, we are / No, we are not_
They _are_ ... -> _Are_ they ...? -> _Yes, they are / No, they are not_
