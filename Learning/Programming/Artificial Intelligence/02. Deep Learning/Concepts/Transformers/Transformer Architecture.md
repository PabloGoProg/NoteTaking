_Transformers_ have two major components (which are separated in modern implementations):

1. **Encoder** -> Processes original text and develops _deep contextual understanding_ of it.
	- Nowadays. an encoder is the represented by an embedding model, since its aim is to develop _rich semantic representations_ of text.

2. **Decoder** -> Generates new text based on the deep contextual understanding of the encoder.
	- Most LLMs only incorporate the decoder, as they focus only on text generation.
