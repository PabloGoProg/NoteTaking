_Reranking_ is a technique that aims to improve the quality of the retrieval after a collection of documents or chunks has already been retrieved.

From [[11. Encoders|Encoders]] we know that some encoding techniques _are much more great in terms of retrieval quality, but at the cost of higher computational costs_. -> So, the idea is to use Bi-Encoding retrieval to `overfecth` the first set of documents and then re rank the retrieval with a much effective encoder.

![[Pasted image 20260607154600.png]]

- The first retrieval must `overftech` documents or chunks, obtaining between 20 to 100.
- Then a costly but effective technique is used (LLM Based Ranking or [[11. Encoders#2. Cross-Encoders|Cross Encoders]]) is applied on the retrieval set.
- The re rank with a "normal" `top-k`.
