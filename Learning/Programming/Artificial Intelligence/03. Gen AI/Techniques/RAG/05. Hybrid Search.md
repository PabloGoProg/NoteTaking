## Ranking and Hyper Parameters

### Reciprocal Rank Fusion

- Rewards documents for being _highly ranked_ on both searches.
- Allow to control the weight of KW Search vs. Semantic Search.
- Score points equal to the reciprocal of ranking.

$$
\text{RRF}(d) = \sum_{r \in R} \frac{1}{k + \text{rank}_r(d)}  
$$
### Hyper Parameters

1. `k` -> In the _RRF_ formula, `k` is used to control the impact on the most rated documents. for example `k = 0` gives 1 point to the first document while gives $1/10$ point to the tenth. -> When `k = 50`, the first document receive $1/50$ and the tenth $1/60$ points.
2. `b` -> Assign importance or weight to the rankings produce by either the semantic of the kw search.
