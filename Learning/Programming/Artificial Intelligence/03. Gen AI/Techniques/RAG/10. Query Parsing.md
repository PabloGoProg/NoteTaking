  
RAG systems are often deployed in contexts where the end user _expects to interact with an LLM in a conversational manner_, as though they're chatting with another person. As a result, _human-written LLM prompts make for bad search queries_.

The idea is to parse the prompt to identify the **intent** and edit, rewrite, or entirely transform the prompt to optimize it for retrieval.
